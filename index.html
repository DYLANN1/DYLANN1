UFO SIGHTINGS IN USA

The map above visualises where every UFO was sighted during 2016. Each red point helps indicate a new state and the dark purple points indicate a sighting. The state is shown whenever a red point is hovered while and the city and full description is shown when a purple point is hovered over. As seen from the map, the majority of sightings are concentrated near the ocean.

This bar chart depicts the amount of sightings at different hours of the day. The hour slider featured can narrow down the hour range. The majority of UFO sightings are seen during nighttime hours.

The wordcloud depicts the number of times a word has occurred in all the UFO sighting descriptions, the pie chart describes the top 6 sighted UFO shapes and their respective counts.
